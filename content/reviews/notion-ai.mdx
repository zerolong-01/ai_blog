## Overview

Notion AI becomes significantly more useful when the surrounding workspace is already part of daily work. The more context your team keeps in Notion, the better the AI layer tends to feel.

## Best fit

- Teams centralizing notes and docs
- People summarizing meetings often
- Knowledge work that depends on quick internal search

## Limits

If your documents are spread across too many tools, Notion AI loses a lot of its advantage. It is strongest as an in-workspace assistant, not as a universal AI hub.

## Final take

For committed Notion teams, this is one of the cleanest AI upgrades available because it removes context switching instead of adding more software.
