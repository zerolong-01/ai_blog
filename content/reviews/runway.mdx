## Overview

Runway is appealing because it turns multiple AI video tasks into one reasonably accessible workspace. It lowers the barrier for teams that want to experiment without a full studio pipeline.

## Best use cases

- Social media content testing
- Lightweight motion graphics
- Rapid creative prototypes

## Tradeoffs

Credit-based usage can add friction, and quality varies depending on the specific generation or editing task.

## Final take

Runway is strongest for speed-oriented teams that care more about iteration than about traditional post-production depth.
