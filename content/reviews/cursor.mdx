## Overview

Cursor is one of the clearest examples of AI moving from "assistant" to "active coding workflow." The repo-aware editing loop is what makes it feel meaningfully different.

## Best for

- Developers shipping frequently
- Teams navigating medium-to-large codebases
- People who want chat, edits, and code context in one place

## Risks

The speed boost is real, but so is the temptation to accept output too quickly. Review discipline still matters.

## Final take

Cursor is a strong fit for developers who want AI embedded directly inside how they already build and refactor software.
