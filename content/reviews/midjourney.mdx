## Overview

Midjourney remains one of the easiest ways to get images that feel stylish with relatively little prompt wrangling. It is especially useful in early-stage creative exploration.

## Strong points

- Mood and tone are often strong on the first pass
- Great for moodboards and concept discovery
- Distinctive visual identity compared to more neutral tools

## Limitations

Teams that need precise brand control, repeatable design systems, or strict production templates may want a workflow with more explicit controls.

## Final take

Midjourney is best treated as a creative partner for exploration rather than a rigid production engine.
